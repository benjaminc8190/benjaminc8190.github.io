Ricegrades

Context: The “Freedom Project” for SEP11 (Software engineering program class eleventh grade) is a project made to showcase what we have been learning throughout the year, javascript. Additionally, the class had to pick a tool to learn on our own. My partners and I chose React and Firebase.


Content: My partners, Samuel Sharivker and Zixuan Yu, delegated one of us to learn React while the other learns Firebase along with a leader who knows both to make sure that we smoothly integrate each others ideas without conflict.


Challenge: Even though React has a lot of free online resources that could be used to learn, I found it pretty difficult at first because of the extensive prior javascript knowledge I have not yet to acquire. Thus, I must solidify a skill that the SEP curriculum strives to help us master, learn how to read documents. Reading documents are very differnet from reading a novel as you are trying to acquire information without fully understanding the "story."


Takeaway: This project has pushed me passed my comfort zone helping learn how to read documents and becoming more comfortable with what I do not know.


Next steps: With the last year of my school's Software Engineering Program coming very soon, I must continue to push myself pass my comfort zone to continue learning a complex language that I am unfamiliar with.
